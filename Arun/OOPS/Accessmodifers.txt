
private : Private members of a class can't be accessed out side of the class,
	  they can't be inherited.
protected : Protected members of class can't be accessed out side of the class.
	    They can be Inherited.
public    : public members of class can be accessed outside of the and out side of the
	    namespace.
	    They can be Inheritance.
internal : Internal members of a class can be accessed outside class but not side of the
	   namespace.
	   They can be Inherited.
